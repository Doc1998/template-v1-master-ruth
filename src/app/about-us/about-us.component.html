About Us

Ruth O'Connell

I am a professional Irish artist and fashion illustrator.

As a contemporary fine artist, my work is characterised by a deep interest in psychology, literature, religion and mythology. My art has an ethereal element to it which encourages the viewer to reflect beyond their material selves and to delve deeper into areas such as human relationships and psychology, the symbolism embedded in our daily lives, our landscapes, literature and the myths, legends and histories passed down to us. I work in a variety of mediums such as oil paint, watercolour and graphite.

In contrast, my fashion illustrations are far more playful in nature. Using watercolours, ink and Prismacolor pencils, I imagine vibrant, feminine clothing and items which express a playful combination of whimsy, vintage fashion and haute couture, intended to immerse the viewer in a fantastical and colourful world.
